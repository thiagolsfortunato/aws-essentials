Auto Scaling
    :Automates process of adding or remove EC2 instances based on traffic demand.

    Lauch Configuration:
        - Template used when auto scaling needs to add an additional server to your Auto Scaling Group

    Auto Scaling Group:
        - All the rules and settings that govern when EC2 server is automatically added or removed.
    
    Pricing:
        - Free to use
