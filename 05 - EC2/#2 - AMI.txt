AMI - Amazon Machine Images

    : Preconfigured package required to lauch EC2 Instances, including OS, softwares packages and requireds settings

    - Types of AMIS:

        - Communits AMIs:
            - Free to use
            - Generally, with these AMI you are just selecting OS

        - AWS Marketing Place:
            - Pay to use
            - Generally comes package with additional, licensed softwares

        - My AMIs:
            - That create yourself.
