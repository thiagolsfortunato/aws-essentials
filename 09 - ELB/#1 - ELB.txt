ELB - Elastic Load Balancer
    :Distribuites traffic between EC2 instances

    Pricing:
        :Free Tier use is NOT available for ELB (Depending on Region)

    Configuring:
        - Each hour or partial hour the load balancer is running
        - For each GB of data transfered through the load balancer
