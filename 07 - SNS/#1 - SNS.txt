SNS - Simple Notification Service

    :Service that allow automate the sending of email or text message

    - Topics:
        :labels and groups different endpoints that you send messages to.

    - Subscribtions:
        :the endpoints that a topic sends emssages to. (email address, phone number..).

    - Publishers:
        :the human/alarm/event that gives SNS the message that needs to be sent.

    - Pricing:
        :Free Tier use is available for SNS.
